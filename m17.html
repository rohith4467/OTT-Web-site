About the Tour

A tour of the USA offers diverse experiences, from bustling cities like New York to natural wonders like the Grand Canyon. Each region has unique culture, cuisine, and landscapes, promising unforgettable adventures across America.

Top Places to Visit

Statue of Liberty

Iconic symbol of freedom with views of NYC's skyline.

Central Park

An urban oasis offering walking paths, boating, and seasonal events.

Grand Canyon Skywalk

A glass bridge with breathtaking views over the canyon.

Mitchell Park

"The Domes," this conservatory features three unique, domed gardens with tropical, arid, and seasonal themes.

Golden Gate Bridge

An architectural marvel with scenic views over the bay

Waikiki Beach

Famous surf spot with crystal-clear waters and sandy shores.

Na Pali Coast

Scenic cliffs and waterfalls along Kauai’s shoreline.

Niagara Falls

This classic boat tour takes you right up to the base of the falls, where you’ll feel the mist and hear the roar up close.

Kenwood

Kenwood is a hidden gem for cherry blossoms, featuring tree-lined streets that bloom vibrantly every spring.
